  • The game has two players, playing in rounds.
  • In each turn, a player rolls a dice as many times as they wish.
  • Each result gets added to his round score.
  • However, if he rolls a 1, the round score gets lost. Then it's the next players turn.
  • The player can choose to 'Hold', meaning the round score gets added to the global score. Then it's the next player's turn.
  • The first player to reach 100points with their global score, wins the game.
